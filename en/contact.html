How appointments work

Simple, personal, and focused on what you need now.

1. Share your needs

Tell us what brought you in, how long the problem has been present, and what you want to return to.

2. Receive an assessment

The first session is used to understand symptoms, movement limitations, and the bigger clinical picture.

3. Start a tailored plan

Treatment and exercise are adjusted to your presentation, goals, and pace of recovery.

First visit

Useful preparation for your appointment

  • Wear comfortable clothing that allows movement and assessment.
  • Bring any imaging, reports, or medical notes you already have.
  • Share previous injuries, surgery, and goals clearly during your first visit.
  • Expect an individualized approach rather than a one-size-fits-all treatment routine.
