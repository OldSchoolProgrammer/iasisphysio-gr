Who we are

A clinic built on experience, care, and purposeful movement.

IASIS brings together physiotherapy and Clinical Pilates in a refined clinical environment where every treatment plan is adapted to the person, not the other way around.

Our clinic

Physiotherapy in Argostoli with a modern and human approach.

The IASIS clinic offers comprehensive physiotherapy with contemporary equipment, long clinical experience, and individualized care. Treatment focuses on injuries, chronic musculoskeletal and neurological conditions, manual therapy, and Clinical Pilates.

  • Clear assessment before treatment choices are made
  • Hands-on treatment combined with therapeutic exercise
  • Support for both rehabilitation and long-term prevention
